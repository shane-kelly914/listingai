Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026 · Last Updated: April 13, 2026

ListingAI ("we," "us," or "our") operates the ListingAI mobile application (the "App") and the website at listing-ai.app (together, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you create an account, we collect your email address and an encrypted password. If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email, and a unique identifier from that provider.

Property Content You Provide. To generate listings, you upload photos of properties and provide details such as address, price, bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, special features, and optional voice recordings. We process this content to generate the listing descriptions you request.

Voice Recordings. If you use the voice input feature, audio is transmitted to our transcription provider (OpenAI Whisper) for conversion to text. We do not retain voice audio after transcription completes.

Usage Data. We collect information about how you interact with the App, including feature usage, crash reports, and performance metrics, to diagnose issues and improve the Service.

Subscription and Payment Data. Subscription purchases are processed by Apple (via In-App Purchase). We receive confirmation of subscription status but do not store your credit card details. Apple handles payment information under their own privacy policy.

Device Information. We collect basic device information (model, OS version, app version, language, time zone) to deliver the Service and troubleshoot issues.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Generate the listing descriptions you request
  • Authenticate users and manage accounts
  • Process subscriptions and prevent fraud
  • Send transactional emails (account, billing, security)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your content to train AI models.

3. How We Share Your Information

We share information only with service providers necessary to operate the Service:

  • OpenAI — processes your property details, photos, and voice recordings to generate listing text.
  • Google Firebase — provides authentication, user database, and hosting.
  • Apple — processes subscription payments.
  • Vercel — hosts our backend API.
  • RevenueCat — manages in-app subscription state (iOS only).

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, or in the event of a business transfer.

4. Data Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active. If you delete your account, we remove your profile data and any saved listings within 30 days, except where retention is legally required (e.g., tax records for completed purchases). Voice recordings are deleted immediately after transcription.

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Port your data to another service
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, email us at support@listing-ai.app. We will respond within 30 days.

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we collect and to request deletion. We do not sell personal information.

EU/UK residents have rights under the GDPR/UK GDPR. Our legal bases for processing are contract performance (to deliver the Service), legitimate interests (to improve and secure the Service), consent (for optional features), and legal obligation.

6. Children's Privacy

ListingAI is not directed to children under 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn we have collected data from a child, we will delete it.

7. Security

We use industry-standard measures including TLS encryption in transit, encrypted storage at rest, and access controls. No system is perfectly secure; we encourage you to use a strong, unique password.

8. International Transfers

The Service is operated from the United States. If you access it from outside the US, your information will be transferred to and processed in the US.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced in the App or by email at least 30 days before taking effect.
